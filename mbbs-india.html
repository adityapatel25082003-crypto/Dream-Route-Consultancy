MCC vs State Counseling

The counseling process is divided into two main streams. Missing a deadline or incorrect choice filling can cost you a seat despite a good score.

account_balance

MCC Counseling (AIQ)

Handles 15% All India Quota seats in Gov colleges, and 100% seats in Deemed Universities, Central Universities (DU, AMU, BHU), and AIIMS/JIPMER.

map

State Counseling

Handles 85% State Quota seats in Gov colleges and 100% seats in Private medical colleges within that specific state.
